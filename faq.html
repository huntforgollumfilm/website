Will the film be available for download?
No the film will only be available for free streaming, and can be viewed in HD at Dailymotion, through this site and will also be launched on other streaming video sites like Youtube. Downloading the film would be a breach of copyright.
 
Do you have permission for this from Tolkien Enterprises?
The film is completely unofficial and non-profit. We have reached an understanding with Tolkien Enterprises to allow the film to be released non-commercially online but the project is completely unofficial and unaffiliated.
 
Was the budget really only £3000?
 Yes - as the crew were all unpaid volunteers (fans like us), the only production costs were for equipment, costumes, props....etc.  And we used digital HDV cameras to keep the budget minimal. It was funded by the filmmakers themselves.
